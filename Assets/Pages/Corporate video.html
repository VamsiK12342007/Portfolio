Pro-Line Promotional Video

As a part of school assignment, I made a prmotional video for any client. Our team of 2, we chose a remote control car company called Pro-Line to shoot our video.

Concept Pitch

The concept pitch was the first thing we did, like explaining our idea and how we will be shooting and edit the video and how the final product will look like.

Script

The second step was to script the video. We used Celtx program to script it. We kept the script as simple as possible and up to 30 seconds only.
