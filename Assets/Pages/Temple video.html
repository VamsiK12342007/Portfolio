Guruvayurappan Temple - Interview with the Priest

This was a school assignment to a interview with a professional of some sort, so with the group of poeple, we shot & edited in video.

Concept

As a group, after thinking a lot, we decided to do the interview on priest at local temple. I had a contact of the priest from this temple,so I was able to set it up the interview. One of the auspicaus festivals, with gathering of poeple, we were able to shoot a good amout of B-Roll.

Equipment

We used school equipment like Nikon D3400 with 18-55mm Lens. We also used manfrotto Tripod as well. And for lighting, we used LED lighting just for the shooting the interview. We also did a lot of hand held filming for B-Roll

Editing

We as a group, the editing on Adobe Premiere Pro. The first version came out to 5.5 minutes, we cut it down to 3 minutes, since the assignment requirment is 3 minutes

Finished Corporate video
