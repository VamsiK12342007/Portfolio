    Guruvayurappan Temple - Interview with the Priest

    In this school assignment we have created an video interview with a professional. So with the group of poeple we interacted while doing the interviewt & edited in video.

    Concept

    As a group, after thinking a lot we decided to do the interview with a priest at a local temple. I had a contact of the priest from this temple,so I was able to set up an interview. During one of the auspicaus festival days with a gathering of many devotees, we were able to shoot a good amout of B-Roll.

    We used school equipment like Nikon D3400 with 18-55mm Lens. We also used Manfrotto Tripod as well. And for lighting, we used LED lighting just for the shooting the interview. We also did a lot of hand held filming for B-Roll

    Editing

    We as a group edited the video on Adobe Premiere Pro. The first version came out to 5.5 minutes and we cut it down to 3 minutes since the assignment requirment was 3 minutes
